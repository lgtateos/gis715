Geovisualization

This course will focus on visualization and interface design for geospatial analytics. With readings from textbooks and visualization literature, we'll discuss the applied science of data visualization, the human visual system, properties of light and color, visual salience, motion and space perception, human-computer interaction, and visual thinking processes.  The course will also include hands-on exploration of free and open source geospatial data manipulation and visualization tools.  Some prior programming experience is preferred, though not required. Open to both Certificate and MGIST students.
